Postdoc at Leiden Observatory

I split my time between working on observational characterisation of high-redshift galaxies and working on ALMA user support and other ALMA-related duties at Allegro ALMA ARC node in the Netherlands. You can find a short summary of a paper I wrote presenting observations of a massive, highly star-forming galaxy in the Epoch of Reionisation in a EU ARC newsletter science highlight article.
